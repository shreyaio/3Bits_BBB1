Funding Opportunities for Women Entrepreneurs

Women Entrepreneurship Platform (WEP)

  • What It Is: Promotes women entrepreneurs through mentorship, funding, and networking.
  • Eligibility: Indian women with a registered business.
  • Support: Access to funding, mentorship, and networking.
  • How to Apply: Register on the WEP portal.
  • Website: WEP

The Udyogini Scheme

  • What It Is: Financial assistance for women in rural and backward areas.
  • Eligibility: Women aged 18-55 in agriculture, manufacturing, or services.
  • Loan Amount: ₹25,000 to ₹3 lakhs.
  • How to Apply: Apply through participating banks or MSME.
  • Website: Udyogini Scheme

Stand Up India Scheme

  • What It Is: Provides financial assistance to women, SC, and ST entrepreneurs for setting up new businesses.
  • Eligibility: Women aged 18+ with a business plan for a new enterprise.
  • Loan Amount: ₹10 lakh to ₹1 crore.
  • How to Apply: Apply through participating banks or online.
  • Website: Stand Up India

Mahila Coir Yojana

  • What It Is: Empowers women entrepreneurs in the coir industry.
  • Eligibility: Women setting up coir-based or coir weaving units.
  • Loan Amount: Subsidy up to 25% of the project cost.
  • How to Apply: Apply through the Coir Board’s regional offices or online.
  • Website: Coir Board
